Admission and Withdrawal Rules


-> Parents must be given to understand that they cannot dictate to the management, but that the management has the right to state the conditions under which they will admit or retain pupils in their schools.

-> No one is to be admitted without a transfer certificate, report card and conduct certificate from the school last attened.

-> The parent/guardian should fill in the school Admission form/School calendar with utmost accuracy and read the rules given in the school calendar. The signing of the Declaration form will constitute an agreement on the part of the parent/guardian and the students, to abide by the rules of the school as laid down in the school calendar.

-> No refund of the money paid will be made after the admission is taken.

-> Notice of withdrawal should be given a month in advance, or else the fee for the following month will be charged. Such a notice should be given by the parent or guardian. Those leaving in April must pay fees for May and June.

-> No transfer certificate will be issued till all dues are paid. Such a certificate will only be given during office working hours and two days after the application has been submitted.

-> No certificate of any kind will be issued without prior application by the parent. For every certificate issued from the office a fee will be charged accordingly. A character certificate will be issued on the basis of his/her regularity, punctuality, behaviour and the pursuit of values gained in school, strictly according to the remarks in his/her school calendar.
